# Competitive Intelligence Autopilot

## Objective
Find the top 3 public examples in a business category, benchmark them, extract reusable patterns, and reconstruct an original improved system.

## Legal and safety limits
- Use public information only.
- Respect robots.txt, terms, and rate limits.
- Do not bypass logins, paywalls, CAPTCHAs, or technical restrictions.
- Do not clone proprietary code, private data, copyrighted text at scale, or brand identity.
- Reconstruct original strategies, layouts, flows, and performance ideas in a new implementation.

## Workflow
1. Define industry, location, offer, and target customer.
2. Discover candidates using search, maps/reviews, directories, social, and public rankings.
3. Select top 3 by visible signals: reviews, SEO presence, offer clarity, conversion flow, content quality, local relevance.
4. Capture public pages with browser/Playwright where allowed.
5. Extract: page map, offer, CTA, form fields, trust signals, review strategy, service structure, social proof, pricing hints, SEO keywords, schema, speed signals.
6. Benchmark against target business.
7. Produce gap report.
8. Produce original improved blueprint.
9. Generate website/content/system plan.
10. Store source, chunks, screenshots, and report in Drive + Supabase.

## Metrics to gather
- reviews count/rating when publicly visible
- SEO title/meta headings
- page speed indicators when available
- CTA count and placement
- form friction
- service coverage
- offer clarity
- proof density
- content depth
- social channel activity
- local landing page coverage
