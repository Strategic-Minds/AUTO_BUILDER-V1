# Universal Business Intake Questions

## Identity
1. Business name?
2. Industry?
3. Location or service area?
4. Owner/operator name?
5. Primary email?
6. Phone number?
7. Existing website?
8. Existing social links?

## Offer
9. What do you sell?
10. Who is the ideal customer?
11. Average sale value?
12. Highest-margin service/product?
13. Fastest-close offer?
14. Main objections?
15. Proof assets: reviews, photos, case studies?

## Website/system
16. Need website, app, dashboard, funnel, CRM, Auto Social, estimator, proposal system, or all?
17. Required pages?
18. Intake form needs?
19. Calendar needs?
20. Payment needs?
21. Lead routing needs?
22. SMS/email needs?
23. Dashboard users?

## Competitive benchmark
24. Top competitors you know?
25. Sites you like?
26. Sites you hate?
27. Required style?
28. Required colors?
29. Required compliance/legal language?

## Automation
30. What should happen when a lead submits?
31. What should happen daily?
32. What should happen weekly?
33. What requires human approval?
34. What should never be automated?
