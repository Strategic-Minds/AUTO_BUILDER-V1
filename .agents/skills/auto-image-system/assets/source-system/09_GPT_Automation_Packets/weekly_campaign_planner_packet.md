# Weekly Campaign Planner Packet

Create next week's concrete image production calendar.

Include:
- 2 full flake epoxy garage visuals
- 1 metallic epoxy interior visual
- 1 quartz commercial floor visual
- 1 stained concrete interior visual
- 1 natural polished concrete visual
- 1 overlay or microtopping visual
- 1 concrete countertop visual
- 2 social story or reel cover concepts

For each row include:
- date
- campaign
- finish category
- space type
- platform
- aspect ratio
- prompt pack
- source image needed
- approval required

Output rows ready for `content_calendar.csv`.
