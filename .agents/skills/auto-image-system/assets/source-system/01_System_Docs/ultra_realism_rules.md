# Ultra-Realism Rules

## Finish Accuracy

Full flake:
- Dense, even flake broadcast
- Random chip distribution
- Correct chip scale
- Satin to gloss topcoat
- Real garage or commercial edges
- No marble veining

Metallic epoxy:
- Fluid pearlescent movement
- Depth and reflectivity
- Soft clouding, ribbons, and highlights
- No impossible mirrored reflections
- Works best in showrooms, salons, retail, basements, bars, lobbies

Quartz:
- Fine aggregate texture
- Commercial non-slip look
- Durable, practical surface
- Often used in kitchens, restrooms, schools, clinics, kennels, locker rooms

Solid color:
- Clean uniform finish
- Industrial or commercial realism
- Correct sheen and slight orange-peel texture when appropriate
- Expansion/control joints visible where realistic

Stained concrete:
- Translucent color variation
- Mottling, veining, natural concrete character
- No fake tile seams unless sawcut pattern is specified

Natural polished concrete:
- Real aggregate exposure
- Burnished sheen
- Joint lines and slab variation
- Gloss should reflect light but not look like water

Overlays and microtoppings:
- Thin decorative cementitious finish
- Trowel movement, subtle texture, tonal variation
- Works on floors, walls, patios, retail, restaurants, modern homes

Concrete countertops:
- Realistic thickness, seams, sink/cooktop cutouts, edge profile
- Natural pinholes or slight mottling
- Proper cabinet support and shadows
- No impossible cantilevers unless reinforced design is specified

## Camera Realism

Use:
- 24mm to 35mm wide interior lens for rooms
- 50mm for detail shots
- Natural perspective
- Correct vertical lines
- Realistic depth of field

Avoid:
- Fisheye distortion
- Floating furniture
- Too-perfect CGI look
- Warped cabinets, vehicles, or baseboards
- Fake text on walls or signs

## Contractor Detail Checklist

Include when relevant:

- Expansion joints
- Saw cuts
- Drains
- Cove base
- Garage stem walls
- Baseboards
- Door thresholds
- Control joints
- Stair edges
- Countertop seams
- Sink cutouts
- Wall texture
- Realistic dust-free finished space
