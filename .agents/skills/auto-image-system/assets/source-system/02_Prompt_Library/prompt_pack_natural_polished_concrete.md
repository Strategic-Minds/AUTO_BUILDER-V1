# Prompt Pack: Natural Polished Concrete

## Modern Retail Polish

Create an ultra-realistic natural polished concrete floor in a modern retail store. Include exposed aggregate, real slab variation, control joints, burnished gloss, overhead light reflections, and clean store fixtures. The floor should look like mechanically polished concrete, not epoxy or wet glass.

## Residential Polished Concrete

Create a photorealistic modern home interior with natural polished concrete floors. Show light aggregate exposure, soft gray tones, realistic joint lines, baseboards, natural daylight, and furniture shadows. Avoid fake marble, excessive reflections, and plastic texture.

## Warehouse Polish

Create an ultra-realistic polished concrete warehouse floor with medium aggregate exposure, burnished sheen, slab joints, overhead LED reflections, and practical industrial setting. Contractor portfolio quality.
