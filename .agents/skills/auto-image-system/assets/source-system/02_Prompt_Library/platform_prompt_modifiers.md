# Platform Prompt Modifiers

## Website Hero

Add:
Wide 16:9 composition, strong first impression, clean negative space for headline, premium contractor portfolio look, no text inside the image.

## Instagram Feed

Add:
Square or 4:5 composition, bold finish detail, strong visual contrast, polished contractor marketing image, instantly understandable finish type.

## Instagram Story / Reel Cover

Add:
Vertical 9:16 composition, finish visible in lower two-thirds, clean space at top for headline overlay, realistic lighting, no fake text.

## Google Business Profile

Add:
Natural contractor project-photo style, realistic room, clear finished surface, believable local business marketing image, no over-editing.

## Sales Proposal

Add:
Clean professional visualization, finish shown clearly, realistic space context, customer-friendly, not overly dramatic, no distracting props.
