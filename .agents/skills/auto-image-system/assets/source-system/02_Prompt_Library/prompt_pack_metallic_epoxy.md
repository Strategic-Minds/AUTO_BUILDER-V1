# Prompt Pack: Metallic Epoxy Floors

## Luxury Showroom Metallic

Create an ultra-realistic metallic epoxy floor in a modern showroom with black, charcoal, silver, and subtle copper pearlescent movement. The finish should show fluid depth, soft ribbons, realistic highlights, and a glossy topcoat without looking like water or a mirror. Include modern walls, furniture reflections, control joints where realistic, and clean commercial lighting. 24mm interior photography, contractor portfolio style.

## Salon / Retail Metallic

Create a photorealistic metallic epoxy floor in a high-end salon or boutique retail space. Use pearl white, smoky gray, and champagne metallic movement with soft cloudy depth. Include chairs, product shelves, natural reflections, and realistic room perspective. No fake text, no logos, no impossible glass-like reflections, no fantasy swirls.

## Basement Metallic

Create an ultra-realistic finished basement with a metallic epoxy floor using deep charcoal, silver, and subtle blue undertones. Include recessed lighting, baseboards, furniture, realistic slab edges, and natural reflections. The floor should feel premium but installable by a professional coatings contractor.

## Metallic Detail Shot

Create a realistic close-up of metallic epoxy showing pearlescent depth, soft movement lines, clearcoat gloss, and natural light reflection. No exaggerated lava pattern, no fake marble, no impossible shine.
