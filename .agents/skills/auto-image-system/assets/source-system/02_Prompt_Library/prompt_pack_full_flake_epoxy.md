# Prompt Pack: Full Flake Epoxy Floors

## Residential Garage Hero

Create an ultra-realistic professional contractor portfolio photo of a full flake epoxy garage floor in a clean two-car residential garage. Use a neutral gray, black, white, and tan flake blend with dense full broadcast coverage and a satin-gloss clear topcoat. Include realistic stem wall edges, baseboards, garage door daylight, subtle reflections, expansion joints, and clean wall details. Shot on a 24mm interior lens from waist height. Avoid CGI look, impossible reflections, fake text, logos, marble veining, or oversized flakes.

## Close-Up Texture Detail

Create a 50mm macro-style detail photo of a full flake epoxy floor showing dense random chip distribution, correct flake scale, clear topcoat depth, slight orange-peel texture, and realistic edge lighting. The image should look like a real installed coating sample, not a digital pattern. No fake text, no logos, no fantasy colors.

## Commercial Shop Floor

Create an ultra-realistic commercial full flake epoxy floor in a clean automotive shop or service bay. Include gray full flake broadcast, high-wear topcoat sheen, floor drains, cove base or wall edge, overhead LED lighting, and realistic tool cabinets in the background. Contractor portfolio quality. No brand logos, no fake signs, no impossible reflections.

## Laundry / Utility Room

Create a realistic full flake epoxy floor in a residential laundry or utility room with washer/dryer, baseboards, door threshold, and even chip broadcast. Use a light gray flake blend and satin finish. Natural indoor lighting, clean installation, realistic room scale.
