# QA Checklist

## Material Accuracy

- Finish category is obvious and correct
- Texture matches the finish type
- Flake, quartz, aggregate, stain, polish, overlay, or countertop details are realistic
- Gloss level is believable
- Scale is correct
- No fake marble effect unless the finish is a decorative overlay/countertop intentionally styled that way

## Installation Realism

- Edges meet walls correctly
- Baseboards, cove base, stem walls, drains, thresholds, or cabinets make sense
- Control joints/saw cuts appear where realistic
- Countertop thickness and support are believable
- Reflections match the lighting
- Room perspective is not warped

## Marketing Readiness

- Finish is clear within 2 seconds
- Image matches platform aspect ratio
- No fake text, logos, addresses, or license plates
- Looks professional enough for contractor marketing
- Does not imply a fake completed project unless labeled as a concept

## Status

Choose one:

- `approved`
- `revise`
- `rejected`

Always write a short QA note.
