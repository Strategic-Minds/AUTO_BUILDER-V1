# Image QA Scorecard

Score each asset 1-100.

- Finish accuracy: 25
- Ultra-realism: 25
- Contractor buildability: 15
- Platform fit: 10
- Brand fit: 10
- Claim/disclosure safety: 10
- Metadata completeness: 5

Anything below 90 stays out of approved folders.

Never approve an image that misrepresents an AI concept as a real completed project.
