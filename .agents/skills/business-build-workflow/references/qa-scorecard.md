# QA Scorecard

Score from 1 to 100.

## Categories

- Frontend quality: 15
- Workflow integrity: 15
- Brand fidelity: 15
- Website-pack fidelity: 15
- Forms and integrations: 10
- Reliability and build health: 10
- Security posture: 5
- Accessibility and responsive behavior: 5
- SEO and metadata: 5
- Documentation and handoff: 5

## Rules

- 100 = enterprise-grade, verified, ready.
- 90-99 = strong but incomplete.
- 70-89 = usable but below enterprise-grade.
- Below 70 = materially incomplete.

Never score 100 if:

- Browser validation was required but not run.
- Build failed.
- User-selected design was not followed.
- Forms are not tested.
- Deployment is unverified.
- Known blocker remains unresolved.

## Report Format

- Score
- What passed
- Defects
- Risk
- Fixes applied
- Remaining blockers
- Next validation required
