# Contractor Content Engine

## Purpose

This content engine is reusable for Nashville Resin Worx and future contractor, construction, epoxy, concrete, wood, stone, and surface-prep projects. It turns research into content ideas, content ideas into scheduled assets, assets into posts, posts into engagement, and engagement into measurable leads.

## Operating Loop

`SEARCH -> SCORE -> BRIEF -> GENERATE -> REVIEW -> PERFECT -> SCHEDULE -> POST -> ENGAGE -> ANALYZE -> OPTIMIZE -> ARCHIVE OR REPLICATE`

## Generator Prompt Template

For every content item, generate:

- channel
- audience
- pain point
- promise
- proof requirement
- visual direction
- hook variants
- caption
- CTA
- image or video prompt
- engagement responses
- measurement hypothesis

## Analyzer Rules

Score every post on:

- reach
- saves
- shares
- comments
- clicks
- leads
- booked estimates
- cost per lead when paid
- asset reuse potential
- brand-fit score

## Perfector Rules

Improve weak content by changing one variable at a time:

- hook
- thumbnail
- first three seconds
- CTA
- offer
- proof asset
- audience segment
- posting time
- format

## Poster Rules

Publishing tools such as Metricool or Xyla must remain approval-gated until the operator explicitly allows auto-publish. Before publish, every item needs status `approved`, channel mapping, final asset link, caption, CTA, and rollback/quarantine path.

## Engagement Rules

Draft replies automatically, but only post externally under an approved response policy. Escalate pricing, legal, refund, warranty, angry customer, safety, and private-info messages to the operator.

## CMS Rules

The CMS must show blocks with exact actions to fix them. Each blocked item needs owner, source, required fix, gate, due date, and next safe action. Quarantine is the trash/recovery area for rejected or unsafe content.
