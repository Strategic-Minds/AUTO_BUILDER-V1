# AUTO BUILDER MVP CLOSED-LOOP VALIDATION PACKAGE

## Purpose
Validate that AUTO BUILDER can operate as a governed universal business-building pipeline with recursive continuity, executive intervention controls, continuity restoration, sandbox-first execution, and closed-loop commerce-content-finance orchestration.

## Validation Goal
The MVP validation package must prove that AUTO BUILDER can:
1. Intake a business or content objective.
2. Research and structure the opportunity.
3. Build governed operational assets.
4. Generate repurpose-ready content.
5. Route social publishing preparation.
6. Prepare launch and attribution.
7. Preserve continuity.
8. Dehydrate and continue recursively.

## Required Governance Conditions
1. PHASE-X / STEP-Y progression.
2. Executive final block reporting.
3. Recursive NEXT GPT INSTRUCTION continuation.
4. Blocker/workaround/self-heal logging.
5. Verified/Inferred/Could Not Verify separation.
6. Sandbox-first execution.
7. Governance mutation lock preservation.
8. Drive/Sheets continuity preservation.
